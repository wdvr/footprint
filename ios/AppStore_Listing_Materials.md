## Competition Analysis & Positioning

### Direct Competitors
- **TripIt**: Focuses on trip organization, not visual tracking
- **Visited Countries**: Simple tracking, limited features
- **Travel Map**: Basic mapping, no smart import

### Competitive Advantages
1. **Privacy-First**: Unlike competitors, no ads or data harvesting
2. **Offline-First**: Works without internet, others require connectivity
3. **Smart Import**: Unique photo and email analysis features
4. **Beautiful Design**: Modern SwiftUI interface vs outdated competitors
5. **Comprehensive**: Countries + states + provinces in one app
6. **Free**: No subscription model, one-time purchase consideration

### Positioning Statement
"Footprint is the privacy-first travel tracker for adventurers who want to visualize their journeys beautifully without sacrificing their personal data."
