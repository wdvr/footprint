Track your travels and mark the countries, US states, and Canadian provinces you've visited on a beautiful interactive world map.

• Track visits to all 195 UN member countries
• Track all 50 US states plus Washington DC
• Track 10 Canadian provinces and 3 territories

• Offline-first with background sync
• Sign in with Apple for secure cloud backup

SIMPLE & BEAUTIFUL:
Footprint focuses on what matters - tracking your travels without feature bloat. The clean, intuitive interface lets you mark visited places with a single tap.

PRIVACY FOCUSED:
Your travel data stays yours. We collect minimal data and everything syncs securely with your Apple ID.
