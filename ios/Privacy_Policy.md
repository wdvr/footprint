# Privacy Policy for Footprint Travel Tracker

*Last Updated: January 27, 2026*

## Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Footprint is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your travel data is personal and should remain under your control. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Footprint travel tracking application.

**The Short Version**: We collect minimal data, store most of it locally on your device, and never sell your information to third parties.

---

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide Directly
- **Travel Records**: Countries, US states, and Canadian provinces you mark as visited
- **Visit Dates**: When you traveled to specific locations (optional)
- **Personal Notes**: Any notes you add to your travel records (optional)
- **Account Information**: If you choose to sign in with Apple or Google, we receive basic profile information (name, email)

### Data We Import (With Your Permission)
- **Photo GPS Data**: Location metadata from photos you choose to analyze for travel history
- **Email Travel Data**: Travel confirmations and itineraries from Gmail (if you connect your account)
- **Calendar Events**: Travel-related events from Google Calendar (if you connect your account)

### Automatically Collected Data
- **Location Data**: Current location when you enable background tracking (for automatic travel detection)
- **Device Information**: Basic device type and iOS version (for app functionality and support)
- **App Usage**: Which features you use (to improve the app)

### Data We Don't Collect
- ❌ We don't track your web browsing
- ❌ We don't access your contacts
- ❌ We don't read your messages or emails beyond travel confirmations
- ❌ We don't use advertising trackers
- ❌ We don't sell your data to third parties

---

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Purposes
1. **Travel Tracking**: Store and display your visited locations on maps
2. **Statistics**: Calculate travel progress and achievements
3. **Import Features**: Analyze your photos, emails, and calendar to find travel history
4. **Sync**: Keep your data consistent across your devices (if you enable cloud sync)
5. **Notifications**: Alert you when you visit new locations (if enabled)

### We Will Never
- Sell your travel data to anyone
- Share your specific locations without your permission
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Track you for marketing outside the app

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage (Default)
- **Primary Storage**: All your travel data is stored locally on your device
- **Encryption**: Data is encrypted using iOS standard encryption
- **Control**: You have complete control over locally stored data

### Cloud Storage (Optional)
- **User Choice**: Cloud sync is entirely optional
- **Encryption**: Data transmitted to our servers is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Server Location**: Data stored in secure AWS servers in the United States
- **Access**: Only you can access your travel data with your authentication credentials

### Security Measures
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Server infrastructure follows AWS security best practices
- We regularly review and update our security measures
- No unencrypted storage of personal data

---

## Third-Party Integrations

### Sign in with Apple
- **Data Received**: Name, email address (or private relay email)
- **Purpose**: Account creation and authentication
- **Apple's Role**: Apple handles the authentication; we don't store your Apple ID password
- **Privacy**: You can choose to hide your email using Apple's private relay

### Google Sign In
- **Data Received**: Name, email address, profile picture (optional)
- **Purpose**: Account creation and authentication
- **Scope**: We only request basic profile information
- **Revocation**: You can revoke access anytime in your Google Account settings

### Gmail Import (Optional)
- **Access**: Read-only access to search for travel confirmations
- **Scope**: We only search for travel-related emails (flight confirmations, hotel bookings)
- **Local Processing**: Email analysis happens on your device when possible
- **Control**: You can disconnect and delete imported data anytime

### Google Calendar Import (Optional)
- **Access**: Read-only access to search for travel events
- **Scope**: We only look for events with location information
- **Purpose**: Automatically detect travel dates and destinations
- **Control**: You can disconnect anytime

### Apple Photos
- **Access**: Read-only access to photo metadata (with your permission)
- **GPS Data**: We analyze location data embedded in photos
- **Privacy**: We don't upload or store your actual photos
- **Processing**: GPS analysis happens entirely on your device

---

## Location Services

### Background Location Tracking (Optional)
- **Purpose**: Automatically detect when you visit new countries or states
- **Precision**: We use "significant location changes" for battery efficiency
- **Storage**: Location data is processed and stored as travel records, not raw location logs
- **Control**: You can enable/disable anytime in iOS Settings or within the app

### Location Data Usage
- **Travel Detection**: Determine which countries/states you've visited
- **Notifications**: Alert you to new places (if enabled)
- **Statistics**: Calculate travel achievements and progress
- **No Tracking**: We don't create detailed movement logs or track your daily routes

### Your Location Rights
- Enable or disable location access anytime
- Choose between "While Using App" or "Always" permissions
- View and delete all location-based travel records
- Export your travel data in standard formats

---

## Your Privacy Rights

### Data Access
- **View**: See all data we have about you in the app's settings
- **Export**: Download your complete travel history in JSON format
- **Portability**: Take your data to other apps or services

### Data Control
- **Edit**: Modify or correct any travel records
- **Delete**: Remove specific travel records or clear all data
- **Account Deletion**: Permanently delete your account and all associated data

### Communication Preferences
- **Notifications**: Control which travel notifications you receive
- **Marketing**: We don't send marketing emails (we don't have a mailing list)
- **Support**: We'll only contact you in response to support requests

### Regional Privacy Rights

#### For EU Users (GDPR)
- **Legal Basis**: Legitimate interest for app functionality, consent for optional features
- **Data Portability**: Right to receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Erasure**: Right to request deletion of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Right to correct inaccurate data
- **Data Protection Officer**: Contact privacy@footprint-travel.com for GDPR requests

#### For California Users (CCPA)
- **Right to Know**: What personal information we collect and how we use it
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: We don't sell personal information, so no opt-out needed
- **Non-Discrimination**: We won't discriminate based on privacy choices

#### For Users Worldwide
- We apply the highest privacy standards globally, regardless of your location
- You have the right to control your personal data
- We'll respond to privacy requests within 30 days

## Children's Privacy

Footprint is suitable for all ages but doesn't specifically target children under 13. If you're under 13, please have a parent or guardian review this Privacy Policy and assist with app usage.

**For Children Under 13**:
- We recommend using the "offline only" mode
- A parent should control any cloud sync or account creation
- Background location tracking should be disabled
- We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent

---

## International Data Transfers

If you're outside the United States and choose to enable cloud sync:
- Your data may be transferred to and stored in the United States
- We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers
- You have the right to know about and control these transfers
- Data protection laws in your country may differ from US laws

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes:

- **Notification**: We'll notify you through the app and/or email
- **Effective Date**: Changes take effect 30 days after notification
- **Continued Use**: Using the app after changes means you accept the updated policy
- **Opt-Out**: You can always delete your account if you disagree with changes

## Data Retention

### How Long We Keep Your Data
- **Local Data**: Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear data
- **Cloud Data**: Stored until you delete your account or request deletion
- **Deleted Accounts**: Permanently removed within 30 days
- **Backup Data**: Securely deleted from backups within 90 days

### Automatic Deletion
- We don't automatically delete travel data (it's your personal history)
- Inactive accounts may be contacted after 2 years of non-use
- We'll delete accounts that have been inactive for 3+ years after attempting contact
